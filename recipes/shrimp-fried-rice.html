Shrimp Fried Rice

Description

Image result for shrimp fried rice description Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat. It is often eaten by itself or as an accompaniment to another dish.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups water
  • 1 ½ cups uncooked white rice
  • 4 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup fresh bean sprouts
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • 1 ½ cups cooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined without tail
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • ¼ cup chopped green onion
  • 4 tablespoons soy sauce
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon sesame oil
